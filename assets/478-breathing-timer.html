🌬️ 4-7-8 Breathing Timer

A manual override for your nervous system

How It Works

The 4-7-8 technique, developed by Dr. Andrew Weil, activates your parasympathetic nervous system and forces your heart rate to slow down.

  • Inhale quietly through your nose for 4 seconds
  • Hold your breath for 7 seconds
  • Exhale slowly through your mouth for 8 seconds

Start with 4 cycles and work up to 8. Practice twice daily for best results.

Tips for Best Results

  • Sit or lie down in a comfortable position
  • Place the tip of your tongue behind your upper front teeth
  • Exhale through your mouth making a whooshing sound
  • Practice on an empty stomach for maximum effect
  • Use before sleep, during stress, or anytime you need calm
