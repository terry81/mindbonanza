About Body Scan Meditation

Body scan meditation is a foundational mindfulness practice that systematically brings awareness to different parts of your body. This technique:

  • Helps you recognize and release physical tension
  • Develops mind-body connection and interoceptive awareness
  • Reduces stress, anxiety, and chronic pain
  • Improves sleep quality when practiced before bed
  • Anchors you firmly in the present moment
  • Reveals patterns of tension you may not notice otherwise

üéØ Getting the Most from Body Scan

  • Find comfort first: Lie down or sit in a position you can maintain
  • Stay warm: Body temperature drops during relaxation; use a blanket if needed
  • Accept what you find: Tension, pain, or numbness are all valid experiences
  • Don't force relaxation: Simply observe; release happens naturally with awareness
  • If you fall asleep: That's okay! Your body might need rest
  • Practice regularly: Daily practice yields the most benefits
  • Try different times: Morning for alertness, evening for sleep preparation
