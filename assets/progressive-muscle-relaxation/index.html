💪 Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Release tension systematically, one muscle group at a time

What is PMR?

Progressive Muscle Relaxation (PMR) is an evidence-based technique developed by Dr. Edmund Jacobson. You deliberately tense each muscle group for a few seconds, then release. The contrast teaches your body what true relaxation feels like.

  • Reduces physical tension and anxiety
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Lowers blood pressure and heart rate
  • Enhances body awareness
Ready to Begin
Find a comfortable position. You'll tense each muscle group, then release and notice the contrast.

Tips

  • Tense firmly but not to the point of pain or cramping
  • Focus on the contrast between tension and relaxation
  • Breathe normally throughout — don't hold your breath
  • Practice in a quiet, comfortable space
  • Best done lying down or in a supportive chair
