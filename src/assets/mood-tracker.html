Why Track Your Mood?

Mood tracking is a cornerstone of mindfulness and CBT. By recording how you feel throughout the day, you begin to notice patterns — which activities boost your mood, which situations drain it, and how your emotions naturally ebb and flow. This awareness is the first step to intentional change.
