Frequently Asked Questions

Are the courses really free?

Yes! All our courses are completely free. Our mission is to make mindfulness accessible to everyone.

Do I need any experience?

No experience needed! Our beginner courses assume zero prior knowledge. If you have experience, you can start with more advanced programs.

How much time do I need?

Most courses require 15-30 minutes per day. The time commitment is clearly marked on each course.

Can I do multiple courses at once?

We recommend focusing on one course at a time for best results. However, you can combine short specialized programs with longer challenges.

What if I miss a day?

Life happens! If you miss a day, simply pick up where you left off. Don't restart‚Äîjust continue. Consistency matters more than perfection.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes! Upon completing certain courses (like the 30-Day Challenge), you'll receive a completion certificate you can download and share.

Can I repeat a course?

Absolutely! Many people repeat courses to deepen their understanding or refresh their practice.

Are there guided meditations?

Each course includes detailed written instructions. For audio guidance, check out our Meditation Timer tool with ambient sounds.
